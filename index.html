Auto-Suggest E-Mail Demo

Auto-Suggest E-Mail is a little handy Javascript tool, that suggests auto-completion to your users, while they start typing their e-mail address in a form. It also has a spelling-correct feature.

Try it now for yourself! You should also test the spelling-correction by entering an incorrect domain name like gnail.com, for example.
